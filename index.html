Javi's life

Javi is seven years old. In the summer, he spends most of his days outside. In the winter, he never leaves the house.

Some things you should know about Javi

  • Javi is always hungry
  • He loves to take a nap
  • Javi is crazy about catnip

Javi's daily routine

  1. Eat breakfast and leave the house
  2. Come back home an hour later and ask for more food
  3. Leave the house to nap/stay at home and nap
  4. Ask for more food and a fuss
  5. Come back for dinner before departing for the night
